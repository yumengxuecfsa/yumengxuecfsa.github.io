核心价值 Core Value

女性的感受重要 Female Feelings Matter

处于婚恋关系中的女性往往受到父权无意识、家庭观念、社会环境等因素的影响，在遭受家庭暴力后选择容忍。

Women in marital relationships are often influenced by factors such as patriarchal unconsciousness, family values and social circumstances, and choose to tolerate domestic violence when it occurs.

为此，我们希望提出“女性的感受重要”这一概念，在成为妻子、女友之前，她首先是一个人，她的需求、安全需要得到保障，拥有在婚恋关系中免于恐惧、担忧、害怕的权利。

For this reason, we want to introduce the concept of “Female Feelings Matter". Before becoming a wife or a girlfriend, she is first and foremost a human being, whose needs and security need to be safeguarded and who has the right to be free from fear, worry and dread in a marriage relationship.

我们是谁 Who Are We?

来自武汉大学新闻与传播学院 From the School of Journalism and Communication, Wuhan University

我们是武汉大学新闻与传播学院网络传播系的三名学生。出于对女性议题的关注，依托学院资源，创建了这个旨在为女性反家暴提供帮助的网站。

We are three students from the Department of Communication, School of Journalism and Communication, Wuhan University. Out of concern for women's issues, and relying on the resources of the college, we created this website aiming to provide help for women against domestic violence.
