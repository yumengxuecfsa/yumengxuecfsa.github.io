什么是家庭暴力？ What Is Domestic Violence?

根据2016年3月1日生效的《中华人民共和国反家庭暴力法，家庭暴力是“家庭成员之间以殴打、捆绑、残害、限制人身自由以及经常性谩骂、恐吓等方式实施的身体、精神等侵害行为”。并将其延伸到“家庭成员以外共同生活的人之间实施的暴力行为”。进一步扩大了反家暴法的保护范围。 According to the Law of the People's Republic of China Against Domestic Violence, which came into effect on 1 March 2016, domestic violence is "an act of physical or mental abuse between family members by beating, tying up, mutilation, restricting personal freedom, as well as regular verbal abuse and intimidation". It also extends to "acts of violence committed between persons living together other than family members". This further extends the scope of protection of the law against domestic violence.

自20世纪70年代第二波女权主义运动将家庭暴力问题作为一项犯罪而不是私人事务推上政治议程以来，家庭暴力在大多数国家已经被认为是必须解决的社会问题与公共政策议题。 Since the second wave of feminist movements in the 1970s put the issue of domestic violence on the political agenda as a crime rather than a private matter, domestic violence has been recognised as a social and public policy issue that must be addressed in most countries.

长期以来，国外性别政策将家庭暴力定义为社会公正的性别议题，强调基于性别的暴力根植于不平等的性别权力关系，以致破坏女性充分行使经济、社会、政治和文化等公民权利的能力和机会。家庭暴力定义被认为是“根源于不平等的性别权力关系和不平等的资源享有的结构性问题”。 Gender policy abroad has long defined domestic violence as a gender issue of social justice, emphasising that gender-based violence is rooted in unequal gender power relations that undermine women's ability and opportunities to fully exercise their economic, social, political and cultural civil rights. Domestic violence has been defined as "a structural problem that is rooted in unequal gender power relations and unequal access to resources".
